# Downforce Technologies — Research Brief

## Executive Summary

**Downforce Technologies** is a UK-based climate-tech startup focused on soil organic carbon (SOC) measurement and land management analytics. Founded in 2021 and headquartered in London, the company combines satellite imagery with ground-based data to provide accurate, scalable, and affordable soil carbon measurement — targeting the agricultural, food & beverage, and fashion/textile supply chains.

---

## 1. Business Model & Core Offerings

### What They Do
- **Primary Product**: Nature Analytics Platform that measures Soil Organic Carbon (SOC) with high accuracy without physical site visits
- **Technology**: Fuses satellite data, environmental surveys, and ground-based measurements using ML/AI models
- **Resolution**: 10m² grid resolution with historical analysis dating back to 2017
- **Key Value Props**:
  - Eliminates labor-intensive soil sampling
  - Reduces cost from ~$30 to <$3 per hectare per year
  - Provides real-time carbon storage data and "realisable potential" predictions
  - Enables Scope 3 emissions reporting aligned with GHG Protocol, SBTi FLAG, ISO 14064

### Revenue Model
- SaaS platform licensing to enterprises, financial institutions, and supply chain operators
- Partnership program for tech integrators and agricultural service providers

## 5. Competitive Positioning

### Australian Market Context
- **Total Addressable Market**: 541 million tonnes of carbon sequestration potential in Australian soil (equivalent to 18 years of agricultural sector emissions)
- **Farmers manage**: >50% of Australian landmass (~2% of global land mass)

### Competitive Advantages vs. Traditional Methods
| Method | Cost | Accuracy | Scalability |
|--------|------|----------|-------------|
| Traditional Soil Sampling | ~$30/ha | High (point) | Poor |
| Digital Soil Mapping | Variable | Low-Medium | Good |
| **Downforce** | **<$3/ha** | **High** | **Excellent** |

### Key Differentiators
- Only solution capable of delivering UK-wide baseline "in under a year" (scalability claim)
- Tier 3 site-specific data compliant with new LSRS standards (validates remote sensing approaches)
- No physical site visits required
- 6+ years historical data capability

### Australian Competitors
- **Loam Bio** (also CEFC-backed, bio-sequestration focus)
- **AgriWebb** (digital livestock platform, CEFC portfolio)
